About Norway

Norway is a Scandinavian country known for its mountains, glaciers, and deep coastal fjords. Oslo, the capital, is a city of green spaces and museums. Preserved 9th-century Viking ships are displayed at Oslo's Viking Ship Museum.

Norway is also famous for the Northern Lights (Aurora Borealis), which can be seen in the northern parts of the country during winter months. The country maintains a combination of stunning natural beauty and high standards of living.

Fun Facts

Norway has the world's longest road tunnel, the Lærdal Tunnel, which is 24.5 km long.

Norwegians consume the most pizza in the world per capita, after the United States.

The Nobel Peace Prize ceremony is held annually in Oslo.

Norway introduced salmon sushi to Japan in the 1980s.
